I started in health tech, then moved through DevOps, site reliability, platform engineering, and most recently AI infrastructure — currently a Senior Platform & AI Infrastructure Engineer at Careem, where my work spans Rails monoliths, Kubernetes and Kafka at scale, and LLM agent platforms used by hundreds of engineers daily.

- ⚙️ Focused on platform engineering, distributed systems, and AI infrastructure that's actually useful in production.
